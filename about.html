Meet Arielle — The Heart Behind T'Ari

Welcome to T'Ari — where every dessert is made with creativity, comfort, and a little extra sweetness. From custom cakes and cupcakes to dessert tables and catered treats, we create beautiful, flavorful desserts that make every celebration feel special. Baked with love, styled with care, and always made to impress.

How It Started

Baking started as more than just a hobby for me — it became my outlet. It’s where I can slow down, be creative, and turn simple ingredients into something beautiful that brings people joy. Although I began teaching myself by watching videos and practicing on my own, I’ve always had a love for baking since I was young. What started as curiosity quickly became a passion, and now every dessert I create is a reflection of the creativity, comfort, and happiness baking gives me.
